Southern Ocean

Aim: Examine if the observed change in oxygen over the observational period is consistent with the ventilation changes documented by Waugh et al., 2013. Use the GFDL ESM2Mc to establish the relationship between ideal age and oxygen in the Southern Ocean. Using the age changes found in Waugh et al., 2013, determine if the observed change in oxygen is consistent with the changes in circulation. Expectation that in SAMW where the age decreases, oxygen concentration will increase. In AABW where age increases, oxygen concentration will decrease.
