About Me

I first became interested in climate science during a high school project on the ozone hole. I completed a degree in meteorology at the Pennsylvania State University where my interest in atmospheric and oceanic dynamics grew. During the past 4 years I have been pursuing my PhD and expanding my data analysis, data visualization, scientific writing, and presentation skills at Johns Hopkins University.
